Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Marbella International Football Academy (hereinafter referred to as "the Academy") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of its users, including minors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information obtained through our website www.academymarbella.com (hereinafter referred to as "the Site").

2. Data Controller

The entity responsible for processing personal data is Marbella Sport Academy S.L., registered at calle Vázquez Clavel, 23, Marbella, Málaga, Spain, with CIF B75356485.

For any inquiries related to personal data processing, you may contact us via email at admin@academymarbella.com.

3. Data We Collect

The Academy may collect the following personal data:

  • Identification data: Name, surname, address, email, phone number.
  • Payment data: Information required to process payments (credit card number, expiration date, etc.).
  • Minor’s data: Name, surname, date of birth, nationality, sports participation details.
  • Guardian’s contact data: Name, surname, and contact information of the parent or legal guardian.
  • Participation data: Information related to involvement in Academy programs, events, and training sessions.

4. Purpose of Data Processing

Collected personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • Registration and participation in sports programs.
  • Sending information about events, promotions, and Academy news.
  • Administrative and accounting processes.
  • Compliance with legal obligations regarding minor protection.

5. Legal Basis for Data Processing

Legal bases for data processing include:

  • User consent (or that of a parent/guardian for minors).
  • Performance of a contract entered into with the user.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected and for the legally required duration.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data when no longer necessary.
  • Restrict data processing.
  • Transfer your data to another provider.
  • Parents/guardians have similar rights regarding minors’ data.
  • Requests can be sent via email to admin@academymarbella.com.

8. Data Security

The Academy implements technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, loss, or destruction.

The Academy reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Any updates will be published on the Site.

10. Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by Spanish and European data protection laws.
