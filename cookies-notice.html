Cookies Notice

At Marbella International Football Academy, we use cookies on our website www.academymarbella.com (the "Website") to ensure its proper functionality and improve user experience. This policy explains what cookies we use, their purpose, and how you can manage them.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our Website. These cookies help us improve navigation, remember your preferences, and collect information about website usage.

2. Cookies Used on This Website

Our Website only uses essential and analytical cookies to ensure its proper operation and enhance user experience.

Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the Website to function properly, enabling basic features like navigation and access to secure areas.

Performance or Analytical Cookies: These cookies help us analyze website traffic and performance, allowing us to improve functionality and content. They do not collect personally identifiable information.

Currently, we do not use third-party or advertising cookies..

3. Consent and Cookie Management

When you visit our Website, you will be informed about cookie usage via a pop-up notification. You can accept all cookies, reject them, or customize your preferences.

You can change your preferences at any time through the "Cookie Settings" option available on our Website.

4. How to Delete and Block Cookies?

If you want to delete or block cookies, you can do so through your browser settings. Below are links to instructions for popular browsers:
