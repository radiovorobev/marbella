Discover Our New Training Programmes Suitable For All Ages

La Academia is pleased to announce the launch of its official training programmes, designed to guide young footballers through every stage of their development. Our structured approach provides the technical, tactical, physical, and mental preparation needed to help players reach their full potential.

Programme Levels

Inicio / Formación (Level C) (Ages 6–10)

This is an introduction to football, where young players learn the basics while developing a love for the game. Through engaging and fun exercises, they improve coordination, balance, and ball control. Training sessions also focus on teamwork and communication, helping children understand the importance of playing together.

Avanzado / Tecnificación (Level B) (Ages 11–13)

At this stage, training becomes more technical and tactical. Players refine their ball mastery, positional awareness, and game intelligence. Sessions also incorporate physical conditioning to build strength and endurance, providing a solid foundation for more advanced training.

Cualificación / Rendimiento (Level A) (Ages 14–15)

The advanced programme is designed for players preparing for competitive football. Training sessions include high-intensity drills, match analysis, and tactical decision-making exercises to equip athletes for higher levels of the game. The focus is on developing professional-level skills, mental resilience, and strategic understanding.

Why Choose Our Programmes?

  • Structured development from foundational skills to competitive performance.
  • Experienced coaching staff with UEFA Pro licenses.
  • Training methods based on the latest football development principles.
  • We are flexible: if a young player’s level is higher than his initial age group, we can adjust his programme.

Our programmes offer more than just football training – they provide a solid foundation for young athletes to grow, learn, and succeed both on and off the pitch.

Enrollment is now open. Join us today via sending us an email or texting via WhatsApp and experience a training environment where passion meets performance.
