Train on Natural Grass at La Academia

At La Academia — Marbella International Football Academy, we are committed to providing young players with the best possible training environment. That’s why we are the only football academy in Marbella with professional natural grass pitches—a key feature that sets us apart and gives our players a significant advantage in their football development.

Why Training on Natural Grass Matters

Natural grass is the preferred playing surface for professional football worldwide. Unlike artificial turf, which can be harsh on joints and increase injury risks, natural grass offers a softer, more forgiving surface, reducing stress on young athletes’ bodies.

Key Benefits of Training on Natural Grass

  • Better ball control and real-game experience – Training on natural grass mimics professional match conditions, allowing players to develop accurate ball control, passing, and movement.
  • Reduced risk of injuries – The softer surface helps prevent joint strain, muscle fatigue, and turf burns, ensuring safer long-term development for young athletes.
  • Improved movement and adaptability – Players become accustomed to the dynamic nature of natural grass, learning how to adjust their footwork and balance as pitch conditions change.
  • By training in an environment that closely mirrors professional stadiums, our players gain the skills and confidence needed to succeed at the highest levels of the game.

State-of-the-Art Facilities for Ultimate Player Development

La Academia boasts two premium natural grass pitches, maintained to elite standards to ensure a consistent, high-quality playing surface. These fields provide optimal training conditions, helping players refine their technique in a comfortable and professional setting.

More Than Just a Pitch – A Complete Training Hub

Our world-class football facility includes:

  • Modern locker rooms – Fully equipped to provide young players with comfort and convenience, fostering team spirit and professionalism.
  • Dedicated viewing area – Parents can watch their children train in a relaxed setting, enjoying Marbella’s beautiful surroundings.
  • Ample parking facilities – Ensuring easy access for families and visitors.
  • Specialized training zones – Designed for both team exercises and individual skill development, allowing players to sharpen their technical abilities under the guidance of experienced coaches.

A Professional Pathway for Young Athletes

At La Academia, we don’t just train players—we prepare them for the future. Our elite coaching team combines UEFA Pro-licensed expertise with a deep understanding of youth development, ensuring that every session is structured to maximize growth, improve performance, and build confidence.

By offering natural grass training, top-tier coaching, and a professional environment, we provide young athletes with the tools they need to succeed at the highest levels of football.
