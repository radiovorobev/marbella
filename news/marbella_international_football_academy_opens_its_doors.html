Marbella International Football Academy Opens Its Doors

We are excited to announce the official launch of Marbella International Football Academy, the premier destination for elite football training in Spain. Our academy is dedicated to nurturing talent at all levels, providing world-class coaching and top-tier facilities to help young players achieve their full potential.

Elite Training for All Ages and Levels

Our academy welcomes players of all ages and skill levels, offering a comprehensive development program rooted in Spain’s renowned football heritage. Through expert coaching, we focus on technical, physical, and mental growth, ensuring that every player enhances their abilities and progresses toward their goals.

Train on Natural Grass Pitches

We are proud to be the only football academy in Marbella with two professional-grade natural grass pitches, maintained to elite standards. Training on natural grass improves ball control, movement, and injury prevention—key advantages that prepare players for the demands of professional football.

Expert Coaching Team

Our team is led by Sporting Director Juan Carlos Añón and Head Coaches Henrique Catanha and Javi Munoz, both UEFA Pro-licensed coaches with extensive experience in professional football and youth development. Their guidance ensures a structured and effective training approach tailored to each player’s needs.

Tailored Training Programmes

We offer structured training across three key levels:

  • Inicio / Formación (Ages 6–10): Fundamental skill development and motor coordination.
  • Avanzado / Tecnificación (Ages 11–13): Advanced technical training and tactical understanding.
  • Cualificación / Rendimiento (Ages 14–15): High-intensity training for competitive football development.

Exclusive Benefits of Marbella International Football Academy

  • Professional Training Environment – Players receive expert coaching in a structured, high-performance setting.
  • Natural Grass Pitches – Our academy provides a professional playing surface to enhance skill development.
  • Spanish Football Philosophy – We integrate Spain’s tactical and technical excellence into our training methodology.
  • Personalised Development Plans – Every player receives tailored coaching to maximise their growth.

Upcoming Events and Opportunities

To celebrate our launch, we are excited to offer free open training sessions, exclusive training camps, and celebrity meet-and-greet events with former and current football professionals. These experiences provide young players with valuable insights and exposure to elite-level football.

Learn More and Get Started

Explore our detailed training programs and upcoming events on the Academy Programmes section of our website. Join Marbella International Football Academy today and take the first step toward reaching your football dreams!
