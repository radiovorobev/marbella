Alvaro Ruiz Joins La Academia as New Commercial Director

We are thrilled to welcome Alvaro Ruiz as the new Commercial Director of La Academia — Marbella International Football Academy!

With a wealth of experience in football operations and management, Alvaro brings to La Academia a deep understanding of the game and a strong track record of working with some of the world’s top clubs and national teams.

Previously, Alvaro served as Coordinator and Director of Operations at Marbella Football Center, one of the leading training camp organizations globally. During his tenure, he was responsible for overseeing training camps for elite teams such as Manchester United, Borussia Dortmund, Real Betis, Belgium National Team, Sweden National Team, and many others. His expertise in strategic planning, operations, and partnerships will play a crucial role in expanding La Academia’s reach and enhancing the experience we offer to young players.

Beyond his management experience, Alvaro is deeply connected to the sport as a former player, having competed in Spain’s 4th Division and Gibraltar’s 1st Division. His firsthand knowledge of the football journey, combined with his business acumen, makes him the perfect addition to our leadership team.

With Alvaro on board, we are confident that La Academia will continue to grow, providing top-tier training, development opportunities, and a professional pathway for the next generation of football stars.
