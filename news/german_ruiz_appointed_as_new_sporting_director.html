Germán Ruiz Appointed as New Sporting Director at La Academia

La Academia is proud to announce the appointment of Germán Ruiz as our new Sporting Director.

A former professional player with experience at top Spanish academies such as Cádiz CF, Málaga CF, and Valencia CF, Germán brings a wealth of knowledge both on and off the pitch. He was part of the Spanish U19 national team under Luis de la Fuente and played in Poland’s top two divisions before moving into coaching and sports management.

Ruiz holds multiple Master’s degrees in sports management, analytics, and rehabilitation. His expertise in player development will be key in shaping the next generation of talent at La Academia.

Welcome to the team, Germán!
