Meet our team

Our academy is led by a team of highly qualified and certified coaches with vast experience in both youth development and professional football. Their expertise ensures that every player receives top-level training, strategic guidance, and the motivation to excel.

Coaching staff

Sporting Director

UEFA Pro, RFEF Sporting Director

Over 30 years in football coaching and development. Over 30 years in football coaching and development. Former Málaga CF Academy Director and assistant coach in La Liga.

Coaching experience in Spain, China, and South Africa, including Hebei Elite (China) and FC Málaga City Academy.

Specializes in football methodology, tactical development, and scouting.

Henrique Catanha

Coach

UEFA Pro

Former professional player for Málaga CF, Celta de Vigo, and the Spanish national team.

Coaching experience includes roles at Zenit Torremolinos, UD San Andrés, CD Nerja, Marbella FC, and Vélez CF. Specializes in high-performance and technical training.

Javi Muñoz

Coach

UEFA Pro

Former professional player at Sevilla FC, Málaga CF, Rayo Vallecano, and UCAM Murcia.

Coaching experience with Rayo Vallecano, CD Lugo, and as head coach of Lincoln Red Imps in Gibraltar.

Winner of the Gibraltar Premier League (22/23, 23/24) and participant in European competitions (Champions League, Europa League, Conference League).

Alberto Montaño

Coach

Marcos Ruiz

Coach
