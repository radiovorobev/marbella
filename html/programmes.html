Programmes

At Marbella International Football Academy, we offer a clear and structured approach to player development. Our training programmes are tailored to different age groups and skill levels, providing young footballers with the technical, tactical, physical, and mental preparation they need to grow and succeed.

Inicio / Formación

Level C Ages 6 – 10

This introductory level focuses on building a solid foundation through engaging, game-based exercises. The goal is to foster creativity, teamwork, and essential motor skills.

  • Learning basic football techniques: passing, dribbling, and shooting
  • Developing coordination, agility, and balance
  • Encouraging teamwork and a love for the game

Avanzado / Tecnificación

Level B Ages 11 – 13

At this stage, players move beyond the basics to develop more advanced technical skills and tactical understanding. Training sessions are designed to help players make quicker decisions and improve game performance.

  • Technical drills for ball control, passing accuracy, and positioning
  • Tactical exercises to build game awareness and decision-making
  • Physical conditioning for endurance, strength, and agility

Cualificación / Rendimiento

Level A Ages 14 – 15

This advanced programme prepares players for high-level competition. Sessions replicate real-match scenarios, focusing on both technical precision and mental resilience.

  • High-intensity drills to sharpen technical abilities
  • Tactical training with match analysis and strategic planning
  • Physical preparation to optimize performance in competitive matches
