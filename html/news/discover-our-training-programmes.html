18 February 2025

We are excited to announce the official launch of our football academy in Marbella, Spain. Our mission is to provide young players with top-quality training in a professional and supportive environment.

Expert Coaching Team

Led by Sporting Director Juan Carlos Añón and Head Coach Henrique Catanha, our UEFA Pro-licensed coaches bring extensive experience in youth development and professional football.

Tailored Training Programmes

The academy offers structured training across three key levels:

  • Inicio / Formación: Fundamental skills and motor coordination for ages 6–10.
  • Avanzado / Tecnificación: Technical development and tactical understanding for ages 11–13.
  • Cualificación / Rendimiento: High-intensity training for competitive football, ages 14–15.

Join Our Open Doors Events

Throughout February, we invite young players to join free training sessions, meet the coaches, and experience our methodology firsthand.
