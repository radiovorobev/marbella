Meet the Coaching Team at La Academia

We are proud to introduce the coaching team at La Academia — Marbella International Football Academy, a group of highly experienced professionals dedicated to developing young footballers through expert guidance and structured training. With a strong background in professional football and youth development, our coaches bring a wealth of knowledge to every session, ensuring that players receive the best possible training experience.

Juan Carlos Añón – Sporting Director

With over 30 years of coaching experience and a UEFA Pro license, Juan Carlos Añón is a seasoned professional in youth development. He has played a key role in the Málaga CF Academy and has worked with clubs across Spain, China, and South Africa. His expertise lies in building strong technical foundations, tactical awareness, and fostering player growth at all levels.

Henrique Catanha – Head Coach

A former professional striker with an impressive career, Henrique Catanha played for Málaga CF, Celta de Vigo, and the Spanish national team. Known for his attacking mindset and game intelligence, he brings his experience to La Academia, helping young players refine their finishing, movement, and overall offensive play. His goal is to inspire hard work and passion in every training session.

Javi Muñoz – Coach

A UEFA Pro-licensed coach with experience in La Liga and European competitions, Javi Muñoz is an expert in tactical development and mental resilience. He focuses on helping players stay sharp, disciplined, and confident under pressure, ensuring they can make quick, effective decisions during matches.

Alberto Montaño – Assistant Coach

With a background in Spain’s Tercera División and Gibraltar's 1ª División, Alberto Montaño has been part of several successful playoff campaigns and promotions. As an assistant coach, he specializes in enhancing players’ technical abilities and game vision, helping them develop a deeper understanding of match situations.

Marcos Ruiz – Assistant Coach

Marcos Ruiz spent 11 seasons with Marbella FC, gaining valuable experience in high-level competition and team dynamics. As an assistant coach, he works closely with players to improve decision-making, positioning, and teamwork, key attributes for success on the pitch.
