The Best Possible Location

The academy features two premium natural grass pitches that meet the highest standards, providing players with optimal training conditions in a comfortable and professional setting.

We are the only academy in Marbella that trains young players on natural grass.

Modern locker rooms ensure convenience and foster a sense of team unity, equipped with everything players need to prepare for training and matches. A dedicated viewing area and parking facilities offer parents a comfortable space to watch their children progress while enjoying Marbella’s beautiful surroundings.

The academy’s training zones are designed for both team exercises and individual skill development, creating a dynamic environment where young athletes refine their technical and physical abilities under the guidance of experienced coaches.

At Marbella International Football Academy, the focus extends beyond football skills. It is a place where character is built, leadership is nurtured, and a deep passion for the game is instilled. Every session is an opportunity for growth, ensuring that players develop both their game and their mindset.
